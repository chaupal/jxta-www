Product: ZIM (Zudha Instant Messenger) Pro
Description:

ZIM Pro is a next generation Instant Messenger [IM] developed using JXTA technology. It is based on Peer-to-Peer (P2P) concepts. ZIM Pro establishes a direct person-to-person connection. ZIM Pro avoids congested public servers and your messages are delivered instantly across the Intranet/Internet. It helps in internal communication within enterprise as other messengers are blocked by the corporate firewall. With ZIM we endorse to prove Zudha's deep Knowledge & commitment in P2P technology.
