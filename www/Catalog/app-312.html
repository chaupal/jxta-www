312 Inc.
Product: LeanOnMe
Description:

312’s LeanOnMe is simple to use, with state-of-the-art Java & JXTA technology that automatically backs up your computer to one or more on-line computers (peer-to-peer). Simply select files or folders to protect and then choose someone you can lean on for remote storage and you're done. No tapes, no CD's, no monthly Web storage fees.
This is more than just a computer insurance policy; you can actually access your data securely from any on-line computer where LeanOnMe is installed. LeanOnMe allows you to create your own group so you can invite your friends, family or colleagues to join. You can use it at home and work and never email, transfer or forget an important file again, just access it remotely.

System Requirements: System Requirements
- 64 Meg of RAM, preferably 128 or more
- 80 Meg free disk space
- Internet Connection, preferably broadband
