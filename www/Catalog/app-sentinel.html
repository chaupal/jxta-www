Dextrus Prosoft Consulting
Services: P2P based enterprise, networking and web-services solutions
Description: Dextrus Prosoft provides technology services and product and project management expertise. Leveraging both open source and internally developed software, Dextrus Prosoft is highly knowledgeable and experienced in J2EE, specializing in P2P, networking and web-services based solutions. Dextrus Prosoft operates in the U.S. and Canada and has a development center in India as well.

Dextrus Prosoft's product is "SENTINEL" - an ideal solution for managing and monitoring the connected enterprise network. SENTINEL is a lightweight tool that is distributed across the network, and utilizes lightweight modules residing in numerous locations around the network. These modules are fully aware of the environment around them and they are intelligent enough to make an informed decision about the problems occurring in their vicinity. They are also aware of other peer monitoring modules around them. And they actively share their knowledge with them.
