Co-Marketing Opportunities for Software Developers

Key Benefits:

  • Higher visibility of your company and products.
  • Recognition for your developers.
  • Highlight your company as a technology leader.
  • Higher credibility with prospects through a closer relationship with Sun.
  • Further JXTA(TM) technology by letting others know how vibrant and successful the community is.
Here are some of the co-marketing opportunities available:

Hosting the JXTA button

  • You can show your affiliation with the JXTA community by putting a JXTA Connected button on your website. Download the button.
  • Requirements - interest in or use of JXTA technology.
  • Getting Started - download the button.

Developer Spotlight

  • We "spotlight" developers working with JXTA technology. When we develop a Developer Spotlight, we feature it on the JXTA home page for two weeks. Then it is moved to the Developer Spotlight Archives accessible from the home page. 
  • Requirements - developer actively working with JXTA technology.
  • Getting Started - Send a request email to marketing@jxta.org then fill out a questionaire and review the article we write.

Company Spotlight

  • We "spotlight" companies using JXTA technology. When we develop a Company Spotlight, we feature it on the JXTA home page for two weeks. Then it is moved to the Company Spotlight Archives accessible from the home page. 
  • Requirements - commercially available product or service using JXTA technology.
  • Getting Started - process should start one month before availability. Send a request email to marketing@jxta.org then fill out a questionaire and review the article we write.

JXTA Solutions Catalog

  • We maintain the JXTA Solutions Catalog to show commercially available products and services using JXTA technology. (We're planning changes for this area.)
  • Requirements - commercially available product or service using JXTA technology.
  • Getting Started - process should start one month before availability. Send a request email to marketing@jxta.org then fill out a questionaire and review the catalog entry.

JXTA Technology Presentation

  • We can include a slide on your product in the JXTA Technology Overview and the JXTA Technical Presentations. We use these presentations for any talks we give on JXTA, they are THE JXTA presentations made available to people within Sun. They are also posted to the jxta.org Web site.
  • Requirements - commercially available product or service using JXTA technology.
  • Getting Started - process should start one month before availability. Complete Company Spotlight, sign a General Publicity Release, and send a screen print to marketing@jxta.org.

JXTA Success Stories

  • Success Stories highlight companies and the applications using JXTA technology. We use the Stories at tradeshows and in talking with the press to explain how companies use the technology.
  • Requirements - commercially available product or service using JXTA technology.
  • Getting Started - process should start one month before availability. Complete Company Spotlight, sign a General Publicity Release, and send a screen print to marketing@jxta.org.

Demos

  • We are often asked to propose keynote demonstrations for shows like JavaOne or provide keynote speakers for conferences like the National Association of Convenience Stores technology conference or Cingular's Wireless Wave conference. We also do technical presentations at industry conferences. We select the applications using JXTA technology most applicable to the audience to talk about or demonstrate.By letting us know about your product, we can talk about it and use it when appropriate.

Exposure

  • In our normal business, we talk with press, developers, and potential customers. We like to talk about and show real-world applications to explain JXTA technology to our audience. We select the applications we show from the companies we know about.

Sun References

  • The Sun Customer References team tracks companies that successfully use our technologies and are willing to be a reference. Giving them the information they need will open more doors to visibility at Sun like inclusion in Sun Software presentations and success story posting.

Press

  • Supporting you by posting and supplying a quote for your press releases.
  • With our press announcements, we like to demonstrate JXTA technology adoption by highlighting the JXTA applications available and in use. You can see the latest example of how we get companies using JXTA technology mentioned in the press.

iForce Co-Marketing

  • If your product runs on the Sun platform, you can also take advantage of our iForce co-marketing opportunities.You can also be listed in the iForce Partner Products Catalog.


For information on how to get started, please send an email to marketing@jxta.org.
