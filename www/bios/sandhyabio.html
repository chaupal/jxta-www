Developer Spotlight


Sandhya Ashok of Bangalore, India, has been a member of the JXTA community since August 2001. She currently performs the role of contributor for the Voice over P2P (VoP2P) project.

She first learned about JXTA while searching for an apt technology to develop a P2P solution for the 'legal professionals' community on behalf of a client. The initial curiosity turned into deep interest and now she spends around 50% of her time on JXTA and related technologies.

Sandhya also contributed as one of the technical reviewers for Sing Li's book "Early Adopter - JXTA" and has learned a lot in the process. She works with a startup software venture named Universal IT SolutionS and is looking forward to riding the JXTA wave to growth.

Sandhya believes that technologies like JXTA and WebServices are natural culmination of man's inherent liking for communication and exchange without middle men (Servers, Websites etc.). She is confident that JXTA is a technology to watch and it would revolutionize the way corporations and individuals interact, in the near future.

You can contact Sandhya at sandhya@unitss.com or on the Project JXTA discuss mailing list.
