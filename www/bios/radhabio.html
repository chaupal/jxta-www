Developer Spotlight


Radha Shreeniwas is a Programmer Analyst at the Innovation Labs in Reuters. Also a member of the Linux community, she is an early adopter of the JXTA infrastructure. Radha is using the JXTA technology to create proofs of concept, drive innovation, and promote awareness amongst engineers and product managers of Reuters, in order to innovate their thinking around the products that target the financial markets.

Radha is active in discussions with the JXTA community and dedicates all of her time to Project JXTA. She has created some proofs of concept around meta - searching and is now trying to develop JXTA for use specifically with financial scenarios (exchange of data, subscription to data and publishing of data).

Radha thinks that the JXTA model is very well thought out and that there are tremendous application possibilities in the area of financial data distribution and exchange. She says that it needs to become more stable as a technology and address important security issues without which it's widespread deployment might falter. However, she sees the JXTA model being adopted in dealing with several key areas of concern in the information delivery business - effective distribution, analytics based on the information and information exchange.

You can reach Radha at radha.shreeniwas@reuters.com or on the Project JXTA discuss mailing list.
