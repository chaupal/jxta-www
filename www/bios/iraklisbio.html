Developer Spotlight


Iraklis Angelos Klampanos has been a member of the Project JXTA community since November 2001. He's a contributor to the JXTA Search Project and spends approximately 70% of his time working with the JXTA technology. Currently, Iraklis is working on upgrading JXTA Search to work under the latest stable JXTA platform. He's interested in distributed Information Retrieval in general and believes that JXTA and JXTA Search are the best place for someone to begin experimenting and researching new techniques.

First and most importantly, Iraklis would like to see Project JXTA remaining free to the public domain. He'd also like it to be widely used in the future by large companies and by individuals in order they improve on their activities. Lastly, he would like to see the project playing a significant role in his personal research interest, distributed and p2p information retrieval.

Iraklis will obtain his Bachelor of Science degree, with honors, in Software Engineering this June and will start working on postgraduate degree in Distributed Information Retrieval next year. You can reach him on the Project JXTA discuss mailing list or at klampaia@dcs.gla.ac.uk
