Developer Spotlight


Sundaranathan S is a Senior Software Engineer for SIP Technologies & Exports Ltd. in Chennai, India. He started working on Project JXTA in December 2002. He is a Contributor for the Instantp2p module and also contributes to the SOAP - JXTA Bindings. He dedicates 30% of his development time to Project JXTA. He started working on JXTA after his project lead gave him an oppurtunity to explore it. He works on JXTA due to his personal interest and cooperation from his company.

Sundaranathan believes that JXTA is a smart architecture and extremely powerful technology for peer-to-peer communication applications. JXTA protocols will become an industry standard for the development of peer-to-peer applications.

He also helps with bug fixes for XALAN opensource. When not exploring new technologies, he plays cricket.

Sundaranathan can be reached at snathan@siptech.co.in.
