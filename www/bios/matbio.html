Developer Spotlight


Mat Dietrich, AIsland Project Owner on Project JXTA from London, UK, spends most of his free time working on AIsland. The project's goal is to provide a framework to build and distribute agents.

Mat works as solutions architect for Internet solutions provider Netdecisions Ltd., building web enabled Enterprise Applications. He believes Agents are a step towards a new Programming Paradigm, which builds on Machine Learning and will one day result in Genetic Algorithms. He would like to see a network of machines win the Turing Test sooner rather than later.

In the early 1970's, Mat started programming by writing Assembler. After finishing high school he became an audio engineer. After a successful career in the German music business, Mat moved to London where he discovered the Internet in 1995 and decided to turn his hobby into his profession.

Mat's vision for Project JXTA is that it will become the standard mechanism for connecting peers. He believes that it is necessary to find an alternative to the client/server model to allow the many devices created from new emerging technologies to talk to each other.

You can reach Mat at mat@mdo.co.uk or on the Project JXTA discuss mailing list.
