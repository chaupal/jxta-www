Developer Spotlight


Sean Kelly of Santa Clara, CA is an independent consultant. He has been a member of the Project JXTA community since May 2001. He is the Project Owner of TINI and a Committer on the Platform, Shell, and JXME projects. Sean's vision for Project JXTA is simple: interoperating in everything, everywhere.

In addition to software contributions to the JXTA platform, Sean provided the first TINI running JXTA in the Project JXTA area of the exhibit pavilion at JavaOne 2002. Bill Joy's vision that any device with a "digital heartbeat" can become a JXTA peer is demonstrated by the TINI running JXTA: small enough to fit in a lightswitch, it can be the digital heartbeat for any appliance or device.

Outside of his work with his clients and on Project JXTA, Sean is the Project Owner of the XML Resume project, and XML/XSL system enabling people to format resumes and curriculae vitae in PDF, HTML, and plain text formats, all from a single XML source. Read more about it at http://xmlresume.sourceforge.net/ He's also the Project Owner of TiniAnt, an extension to the Ant build system for building applications on the TINI.

You can reach Sean at SeanKelly@jxta.org or kelly@kelly.homeunix.com. Check out his web page at http://kelly.homeunix.com/
