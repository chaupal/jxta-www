Developer Spotlight


Susan Entwisle of Melbourne, Australia has been a member of the Project JXTA community since March 2002. She is a contributor to the Platform project. She dedicates approximately 15 hours a week to Project JXTA and is currently working on extending the network management facilities available under the current stable build of the JXTA platform under the supervision of Professor Elizabeth Kendall.

Susan is an undergraduate attending Monash University in Australia on a part-time basis, studying software engineering. She will be completing this degree in November and hopes to continue onto postgraduate research in the area of distributed computing. Susan has several years industry experience in software development in the financial and telecommunication sectors. Currently, she is a technical architect responsible primarily for the design and implementation of web-based applications.

Susan envisions that Project JXTA protocols will become an industry standard for the development of P2P applications.

You can reach Susan at susan.entwisle@bigpond.com or on the Project JXTA discuss mailing list.
