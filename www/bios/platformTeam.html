The Project JXTA 2.0 Release Team

The Project JXTA J2SE 2.0 Release Team is a group of developers committed to delivering high quality implementations of the JXTA technology. Over the past few weeks they've been working very hard on the release. We decided to share little snippets to introduce these team members to you.

They hail from seven different countries.
