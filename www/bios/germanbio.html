Developer Spotlight


Germán Toro del Valle of Madrid, Spain has been a member of the Project JXTA community since it began in April 2001. He is active on the discuss mailing list and files bugs on some of the JXTA projects, mainly the Plaform project.

Germán is working for Telefónica I+D as a R+D Engineer testing and investigating new technologies, amongst which he is mostly dedicated to JXTA. He is looking at new applications of JXTA in fields where P2P has not been traditionaly used.

In addition, Germán is working on his doctorate as a Telecommunication Engineer at the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM). He is currently writing his thesis, in which JXTA will play a main role.

Germán thinks JXTA is an extremely powerful technology although says that every technology needs some time to mature to be established as a general use technology (as did Servlets, EJBs, etc.)

You can reach Germán at germantoro@iespana.es or on the Project JXTA discuss mailing list.
