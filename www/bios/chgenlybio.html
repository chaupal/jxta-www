Developer Spotlight



Chris Genly

Chris Genly really enjoys spending his free time on Project JXTA. He is a committer on the platform and shell projects. He wants to increase his understanding of the JXTA technology and help move the code forward. Chris has been working on an improved message proposal with bondolo and others.

Chris was a Senior Software Architect working on distributed systems at ShareThis. Unfortunately, ShareThis closed its doors in May due to lack of funding. Project JXTA is Chris' first open source project. He likes the result of a good group mind. Chris wants to see JXTA become a widely adopted protocol set for building P2P apps.

You can reach Chris at chgenly@alum.mit.edu or on the Project JXTA Discuss Mailing List.
