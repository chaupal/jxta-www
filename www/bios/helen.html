Developer Spotlight


Helen Chen is the JXTA Community Manager. She develops and supports the JXTA Community in a number of different ways. She works on various aspects of the jxta.org and jxta.dev.java.net Websites with Lauren Zuravleff, our JXTA Marketing Manager. Together, they work to develop site contents to support JXTA Community Members. Helen also provides support for many general community activities, such as monitoring community health, approving the new project requests and also moderating community-wide mailing lists.

Helen is also the Site Manager for java.net. She was previously involved with Sun's Jini Network Technology Community and Sybase's CodeXchange Community. Roles that she has held prior to discovering the world of Community Development include technical support, technical writing, instructional design and performance development. She holds an MS in Instruction and Performance Technology.

Personal interests and hobbies

Helen is married to Steven Harris, a Software Engineer at Idiom, Inc. They live in Framingham, Massachusetts with their rather spoiled cat Jessica. They have 7 nephews and 3 nieces living in New England, Tulsa and Houston.

Favorite activities in quiet moments include music (especially folk and celtic), fiber arts (including knitting and weaving), reading, cooking, and baking.

Life is never boring with so many things going on! :-)
