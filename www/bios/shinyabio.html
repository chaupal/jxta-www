Developer Spotlight


Shinya Ishihara of Musashino, Tokyo, Japan has been a member of the Project JXTA community since May 2001. He dedicates 10-20 hours per week to Project JXTA as a contributor to the Platform, DI, jnushare and GISP projects.

Shinya is a research engineer at NTT Network Innovation Laboratories. Recently his research interest has been in distributed hash tables (DHT). He also established the JXTA Japanese Community " ja-jxta" in June 2002. There are about 140 members now. Everyday they vigorously discuss several JXTA issues in Japanese.

Shinya says that JXTA has a well-thought and smart architecture for peer-to-peer applications. Although JXTA is now at it's naive stage, he thinks that the technology will be the standard mechanism for distributed systems.

You can reach Shinya at shinyability@jxta.org or on the Project JXTA discuss mailing list.
