Developer Spotlight


Gajenthar P T, known as gajentharpt on the jxta.org aliases, works for Zudha in Bangalore, India. He started working on Project JXTA in November 2002 and is a contributor to VOP2P and EZEL projects. He spends about 50% - 60% of his time on Project JXTA.

As an application developer using the JXTA platform, Gajenthar contributes to its widespread adoption through the applications he co-develops with his colleagues at Zudha. He also explains Project JXTA to the local student community through JXTA SIG Bangalore and presents JXME in technical meetings.

Gajenthar believes Project JXTA has the ability to create rich applications which do not require centralized servers. And Project JXTA addresses the vast space of networked consumer and embedded devices, from smart cards, pagers, and mobile phones, to set-top boxes and automobile navigation systems to inter-operate with each other.

You can reach Gajenthar at gajentharpt@yahoo.com
