Developer Spotlight


Radu Muresan dedicates approximately 60% of his time to Project JXTA. He's interested in the platform and security projects. In addition to attending Master's courses at UBB University in Cluj-Napoca, Radu is currently working on integrating some security related items using the JXTA technology in his job as a Java software developer at Alfa Global Solutions (AGS) Cluj-Napoca. Other JXTA specific issues which are of interest at his job are linked to the communication part of the technology.

Radu thinks that the idea that drives JXTA is very powerful, yet JXTA has still a long way to go before it will truly become a standard. There is also work to be done regading the docs, which are severely outdated and optimizing the platform source code. From a developer's point of view, JXTA may be unfamiliar at the beginning, but using the mailing lists one can easily find the answer to (almost) any question. He says the market needs peer2peer solutions, and this would be JXTA's chance.

You can reach Radu on the Project JXTA discuss mailing list or at radu@ags.ro.
