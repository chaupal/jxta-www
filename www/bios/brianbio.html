Developer Spotlight


Brian Sayrs of Kauai, Hawaii has been a member of the Project JXTA Community since March 2002. He is a Systems Engineer with Digital System Resources and one of the the owners of the Bench project on JXTA.org. Brian dedicates approximately 15 hours per week to the JXTA technology and is also working with other engineers within DSR and other organizations to develop JXTA applications.

Brian is currently developing test code to evaluate the scalability and performance of JXTA implementations. He is interested in building very large ad hoc networks with measurable reliability. Brian is currently investigating the scalability of JXTA implementations of "diffusing computations" for reliable information dissemination.

Previous to working on Project JXTA, Brian lead the development of the NALU project on Jini.org and is a member of the Global Grid Forum and gnu.org.

You can reach Brian at bsayrs@globalinfotek.com or on the Project JXTA discuss mailing list.
