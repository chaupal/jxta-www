Developer Spotlight


Stephanie Kaul works for Sun Microsystems as the JXTA Community Manager. She develops and supports the JXTA Community by spotlighting developers, universities, and companies that use JXTA technology; evaluating applications for upcoming demonstrations, writing community member success stories, and keeping our Web sites jxta.org, java.net, java.sun.com, and sun.com up to date with interesting information about our JXTA community, events, and news. She enjoys working on company spotlights, press articles, and application demos. You'll see her evangelizing JXTA technology at tradeshows or JXTA User Group Meetings. Her favorite part of the job is to bring JXTA technology, companies, and developers together.

Stephanie joined the JXTA technology team in December, 2004. Stephanie returns to Sun after a hiatus of running her own advertising company. Her tenure at Sun covered over four years, working with products such as the Sun Internet Mail Server and Sun Calender Server, and managing programs such as the Sun TechDays developer conference, the Developer Relations Speakers Bureau, and the Virtual Evangelist and Evangelist in a Box programs. Stephanie holds a BS in Business Management from the University of Phoenix.

Personal interests and hobbies

Stephanie spends most of her time enjoying her four children, ranging in age from 16 years to the newest baby due in October 2008. When she's not doing that, she can be found training and showing her Best in Show-winning cocker spaniels. She and her husband, Jeet, Sr. Vice President, Client Services Group for Sun Microsystems, enjoy traveling and home remodeling.
