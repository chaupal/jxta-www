Developer Spotlight


Lauren Zuravleff works for Sun Microsystems as the JXTA Marketing Manager. Together with Helen Chen, JXTA Community Manager, she develops and supports the JXTA Community by keeping our Web sites jxta.org, java.net, java.sun.com, and sun.com up to date with interesting information about our JXTA community, events, and news. She also helps co-market commercially available applications and services created with JXTA technology. She enjoys working on marketing projects like the JXTA button, success stories, and press articles. You'll see her evangelizing JXTA technology at tradeshows or JXTA User Group Meetings. Her favorite part of the job is to see or hear about new applications using JXTA technology. The breadth of aplications and creativity of developers is amazing!

Lauren started working on JXTA technology in February 2003. While she spends most of her time working on JXTA technology, she also helps market Sun's Project Looking Glass. Lauren has been at Sun for over six years working in the Advanced Technologies, Java, and Sun Ray groups. She holds an MS in Chemical Engineering from Rice University and moved into marketing over 11 years ago after having to don fireproof clothing at a petroleum refinery.

Personal interests and hobbies

Lauren spends a lot of her free time volunteering with children's activities. She coaches her son's volleyball and soccer team, reads with kids in the classroom, and raises money for the schools through the Mountain View Educational Foundation. She and her husband Bill, a VLSI consultant, enjoy playing volleyball, running, bike riding, and hiking. Lauren's also into genealogy and scrapbooking.
