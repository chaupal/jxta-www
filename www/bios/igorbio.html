Developer Spotlight


Igor Katz of Tel Aviv, Israel has been a member of the Project JXTA community since June 2001. Igor is the Chief Technology Officer at VistaPortal Software and is active in several Open Source technology user groups. He spends approximately 30 hours a week developing VistaPoint, an integrated Web-based reporting and services environment that incorporates JXTA technology to enable a virtual data repository for peer-to-peer collaboration and secure file sharing.

Igor sees peer-to-peer technology, especially JXTA as a base for the emerging generation of Internet applications. These new applications will enable peers (clients in the traditional Client/Server paradigm) to be active in the process of computing. It means that a variety of peer devices including desktops, servers, networks, cell phones and PDAs will share their resources with each other. Igor believes that by accepting that every device be a peer, he envisions a world where rather than using P2P as the terminology, we would use E2E (everything-to-everything).

You can reach Igor Katz at igork@vistaportal.com or on the Project JXTA discuss mailing list.
