Developer Spotlight



Calvin Cheng, known as cxcheng on the JXTA aliases, has worked on Project JXTA for Sun Microsystems in Santa Clara since 2002. He is the Project Owner of project JDF and works on JXTA full-time.

He describes his current work as "entirely with stuffy enterprise server-side software development". He elaborates further "On the other hand, what really thrills me are emerging technologies that can shape our lives. In that sense, I am a believer in decentralized computing, and now that goes beyond sharing music or video."

Calvin's first serious step into JXTA was project JDF where he built a distributed testing framework to help people deploy and execute distributed tests with one command. He remembers this as a nice learning experience in understanding deployment issues as well as the nature of distributed applications. He hopes JDF can become a mechanism and a template to make JXTA better and faster.

He hopes to see JXTA become embedded into existing operating systems on servers, to desktops, to small devices - JXTA Anywhere, Anytime. And then world peace after that. Calvin is also an active consumer of open-source Apache Jakarta projects, and a browser of many others.

Personal interests: Calvin is fascinated by all kinds of gadgets including cars, and how they shape our lives.

Reported October 2003
