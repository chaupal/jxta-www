Naming Conventions

'JXTA' is the name of a collaborative project, not a product or service. Therefore, we recommend that it is used by project participants in a manner consistent with this purpose. 'JXTA' should not be used in the name of any products or service implementing technologies developed as part of Project JXTA.

Sun Microsystems would not object to use of the words 'JXTA' or 'Project JXTA' to describe other collaborative activity taking place within the Project JXTA. While JXTA should not be used as the name of a product or service, Sun would not object to the use of the phrase 'Project JXTA' in product descriptions.

As in any instance where you use a product or service name, we suggest you consult your own attorney and have the name searched for potential conflicts with other companies.
