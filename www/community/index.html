JXTA(TM) Technology Presentations

Last modified August 3, 2005

The JXTA Community is alive and vibrant, sharing information with each other at conferences, regular Town Hall Meetings and other such gatherings. Notes from these presentations serve as a historical archive for our community as well as a resource of technical information.
