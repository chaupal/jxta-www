JOIN US FOR TWO DAYS OF INDEPTH JXTA DISCUSSIONS, 1:1'S WITH JXTA ENGINEERS, AND FUN!

 

Day One - Monday, September 26, 2005; Group discussions

Day one will be a free-flowing interactive indepth discussion session about many JXTA topics. Attendees get to choose which topic they'd like to discuss with the JXTA engineering team.

Some of the topic selections are (feel free to bring some of your own!):

  • JXTA Resources
  • Discovery
  • Resolver
  • Pipes
  • SRDI
  • Rendezvous
  • Relay
  • Deployment
  • Configuration
  • Best practices
  • PSE Membership
  • TLS secure messaging
  • JXTA-C & Java interoperability
  • JXTA-C Query Extensions

Breakfast, lunch, and dinner will provided on Monday.

Day Two - Tuesday, September 27, 2005; 1 on 1's

Meet 1:1 with your favorite JXTA engineers. Have detailed technical conversations about any questions, concerns, recommendations, etc. that you are experiencing with JXTA. Engineers available are: Mohamed AbdelAziz (hamada), Mike Duigou (bondolo), James Todd (gonzo), and Henry Jen.

Breakfast and lunch will be provided on Tuesday.

COME JOIN US! It will be fun! Please RSVP your attendance with Stephanie Kaul (marketing@jxta.org). For those of you who have already contacted her, you're all set!
