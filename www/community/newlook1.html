The new JXTA(TM) technology banner

Since the beginning of Project JXTA, this symbol has come to represent our community.

    

 

Lately, we've been working to refresh our look a bit. You may have participated in the discuss alias thread, where we discussed different "taglines" that we could use. After international trademark searches, we found JXTA(TM) Connected best fits our needs. A graphic artist has taken our feedback and designed the four banners below. We are asking for your vote to select the new banner to represent the JXTA Community.

The chosen banner will be used on the jxta.org Web site. It will also be available for people to use on their own Web sites to show affiliation to Project JXTA. To show the banner on your Web site, you accept a click-through agreement. This agreement makes sure people use it in a proper way (don't modify it, etc...) and link it to www.jxta.org.

Please help us make this important selection by telling us what you think. Please send your comments directly to Lauren Zuravleff, JXTA Marketing Manager at Sun Microsystems.

Thank you in advance for your feedback!
